Green Beans

Simple boiled green beans

A quick and easy side of vegatables.

Quick and cheap to make

Required Ingredients

  • Green beans
  • Butter
  • Table salt
    •

    Recipe Steps

    1. Bring water to a roiling boil
    2. Salt water to the water
    3. Add the green beans to the boiling water (boil all the vitamins outta them )
    4. let boil for about 5 mins at medium heat
    5. drain water and plate
    6. lightly coat with melted butter (optional)
